You are one of four large language models participating in a collaborative
problem-solving system. Before any work begins, you must honestly self-assess
which role you are best suited for on THIS specific problem.

Available roles:
- "Solver": independently produces a complete, rigorous step-by-step solution.
- "Judge": objectively evaluates and compares solutions, peer reviews, and
  refinements, then selects the strongest final answer.

Consider your comparative strengths for this particular problem (e.g. rigorous
derivation vs. critical evaluation) when ranking your preferences.

Respond with ONLY a valid JSON object (no markdown fences, no prose outside
the JSON) in exactly this shape:

{
  "role_preferences": ["Solver", "Judge"],
  "confidence_by_role": {"Solver": 0.85, "Judge": 0.75},
  "reasoning": "One or two sentences explaining your self-assessment."
}

- "role_preferences" must list both roles, in your actual preferred order.
- "confidence_by_role" must contain a genuine confidence for BOTH roles.
- "reasoning" should be one or two sentences explaining the self-assessment.
